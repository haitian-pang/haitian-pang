About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Lifeng Sun . Prior to joining Lab of Multimedia and Networking, I received my B.E. degree from Department of Automation at Tsinghua University in 2014. My research work focuses on edge computing and multimedia network optimization.
